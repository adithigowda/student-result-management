About us

N.M.A.M. Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), full name Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology, is an autonomous engineering college in Nitte, Karnataka, India. It was established in 1986, with programs in computer science, mechanical engineering, civil engineering and electrical engineering. Currently it offers B.E. in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Biotechnology Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer & Communications Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Information Science and Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. It is one of the best engineering college in Karnataka. The college is run by the Nitte Education Trust, which was founded in 1979 by Justice Kowdoor Sadananda Hegde, former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court and former Speaker of the Lok Sabha. The college is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum.
